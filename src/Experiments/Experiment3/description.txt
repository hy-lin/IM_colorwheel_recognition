This is the experiment combined recall and recognition task together. There are three different sessions in the expeirment:
1) pure recall
2) pure recognition
3) combined recognition and recall

The core requirements for the experiment are:
1) set-size manipulation. 1, 2, 4, 6
2) Enough trials for recognition manipulation. 80 trials per set-size. 40 positive, 20 new, 20 intrusion. 

pure recall: 1 session
pure recognition: 1 session
combined recognition and recall: 2 sessions
The session order is totally randomized.
